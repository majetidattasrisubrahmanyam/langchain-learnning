Machine Learning (ML) is a branch of Artificial Intelligence (AI) that enables computers to learn patterns from data and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed for every task.
Basic ML Workflow:

Collect Data
Clean and Prepare Data
Train a Model
Test the Model
Make Predictions

Example:

Input: Previous house prices and features
ML Model learns patterns
Output: Predicts the price of a new house

Common Applications:

Recommendation systems (Netflix, YouTube)
Spam email detection
Face recognition
Chatbots and LLMs
Fraud detection
